Bitcoin-native infrastructure for organizations

The Galoy open source core banking platform is designed for launching enterprise-scale Bitcoin and Lightning applications.

Bria

Our flagship product, Bria is a powerful Bitcoin banking infrastructure that enables businesses to easily manage transactions, accounts and custody of digital assets.

Bitcoin Bridge

Our Bitcoin Bridge enables clients to easily convert funds between Bitcoin and traditional currencies. It's fast, secure and reliable.

Lightning Gateway

Our Lightning Gateway provides businesses with instant Bitcoin payments and the ability to process high volumes of transactions without the need for a traditional banking infrastructure.
